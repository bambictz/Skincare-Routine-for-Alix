Morning Routine

Hydrate, protect and prime. Keep it light — your skin will thank you.

☀️ You'll want to add an SPF to your collection. You're using retinal (in the eye serum) which increases sun sensitivity. A broad spectrum SPF 50+ as your final morning step is strongly recommended.
1
Cleanse
Beauty of Joseon – Ginseng Cleansing Oil
In the morning, just a small amount is enough — no need to double cleanse. Apply to dry skin, massage gently for 20–30 seconds, emulsify with water, then rinse. Your skin's natural oils from overnight don't need heavy cleansing.
Every morning
2
Hydrating Serum
Boring Without You – Make Me Wet™ Aquaporin Hydrating Milk Serum
Apply 2–3 drops to slightly damp skin after cleansing. Press gently into skin — don't rub. The aquaporin technology helps your skin hold onto hydration throughout the day. Milk serums are lightweight and absorb beautifully under SPF.
Every morning
3
Treatment Serum
Medicube – Collagen Glow Booster Serum
Apply 2–3 drops after your hydrating serum and press in. This boosts firmness and gives a healthy glow — perfect as a morning layer before SPF. Great for combination skin as it hydrates without feeling heavy.
Every morning
4
Eye Care
Beauty of Joseon – Revive Eye Serum (Ginseng + Retinal)
Gently tap a small amount around the orbital bone using your ring finger. The ginseng brightens and firms; retinal targets fine lines over time. Use sparingly — a little goes a very long way around the delicate eye area.
Every morning
5
Soothe & Prep
SKIN1004 – Madagascar Centella Ampoule
A few drops patted gently into skin. On combination skin, the Centella ampoule calms any reactive or oily zones while strengthening the barrier — a great base before SPF or makeup. Can be mixed into your moisturiser if you have one.
Every morning
6
SPF — Recommended addition
Add a broad spectrum SPF 50+ here
Apply as the very last step before makeup. Approx. 1 teaspoon for face and neck. With retinal in your eye serum, this step is genuinely important. Some great options: Ultra Violet Queen Screen SPF 50+, Skin Aqua UV Super Moisture Gel, or La Roche-Posay Anthelios.
Every single morning

Night Routine

Double cleanse, repair and nourish. Your skin does its best work while you sleep.

🌿 Keep it consistent. With 6 products, your routine is simple and powerful. You don't need to rotate much — just be consistent every night and your skin will show results within 4–6 weeks.
1
First Cleanse — Oil
Beauty of Joseon – Ginseng Cleansing Oil
Apply to completely dry skin and massage to dissolve SPF, sunscreen, makeup and pollution. Work in circular motions for 60 seconds, then emulsify with a little water until it turns milky, and rinse. This step is essential if you wore SPF during the day.
Every evening
💡 Tip: You'll want to add a gentle water-based second cleanser to your collection for a proper double cleanse — something like the Beauty of Joseon Green Plum Refreshing Cleanser or a gentle foaming cleanser. Until then, rinse the oil cleanse thoroughly with warm water.
2
Soothe & Tone
SKIN1004 – Madagascar Centella Ampoule
Apply generously to clean, slightly damp skin — this is your toner and soothing layer in one. Pat gently all over face and neck. The Centella asiatica calms inflammation, repairs the skin barrier and preps skin for the serums to follow. Excellent for combination skin.
Every night
3
Hydrating Serum
Boring Without You – Make Me Wet™ Aquaporin Hydrating Milk Serum
Apply 2–3 drops and press into skin. Overnight, the aquaporin technology helps maintain deep hydration levels — waking up to plump, balanced skin. Particularly good for the drier zones on combination skin.
Every night
4
Treatment Serum
Medicube – Collagen Glow Booster Serum
Apply after your hydrating serum and press in. Overnight, collagen-boosting ingredients work to improve firmness and elasticity. Consistent nightly use is where you'll really see anti-ageing results build over time.
Every night
5
Eye Care
Beauty of Joseon – Revive Eye Serum (Ginseng + Retinal)
Gently tap around the orbital bone with your ring finger. Night is when retinal works best — it accelerates cell turnover and targets fine lines while you sleep. Use a small amount and avoid getting too close to the lash line.
Every night
6
Optional — Gua Sha Ritual
Jade Gua Sha Stone
After your serums, apply a small amount of the Make Me Wet serum or collagen serum as a slip agent. Use the gua sha to massage your face — this is the perfect time for your ritual. See the Gua Sha tab for the full technique guide.
2–3× per week or as desired

Gua Sha Ritual

Your jade gua sha is one of the most valuable tools in your routine. Used correctly, it's a genuine anti-ageing treatment.

🌿 What gua sha does for you: Stimulates lymphatic drainage to reduce puffiness, increases circulation for a natural glow, lifts and sculpts facial contours over time, reduces tension in jaw and brow muscles, and helps your serums absorb more deeply.
Step-by-step technique guide
When: PM routine, after applying your serums (the serum acts as slip). Store your jade stone in the fridge for an extra depuffing effect — especially beautiful in the morning for 5 minutes if you have time.

Pressure: Light to medium — you should feel gentle resistance, not pain. Never drag on dry skin.
1
Décolletage first — always start here to open lymph drainage pathways. Use the flat side, sweep from chest up toward collarbone, 3 passes each side.
2
Neck — sweep upward from collarbone to jawline along the sides of the neck. 3–5 slow strokes each side.
3
Jawline — use the curved notch of the stone. Start at chin, sweep along jaw toward earlobe. 3–5 strokes each side. Hold skin taut with other hand.
4
Cheeks — flat side of stone. Sweep from the nose outward toward the ear. 3–5 strokes. Great for lifting and sculpting over time.
5
Under eyes — use the small curved edge very gently. Sweep outward from inner corner toward temple. 2–3 strokes. The jade's coolness helps with puffiness here.
6
Forehead — sweep upward from brow toward hairline, then outward toward temples. 3–5 strokes. Relieves tension and prevents lines from deepening.
7
Finish at temples — use circular motion to release. Then sweep down the neck again to flush lymph toward collarbone. Always end with downward neck strokes.
Care for your jade stone
Rinse with warm water and mild soap after every use. Pat dry. Store away from direct sunlight. Pop it in the fridge 10–15 min before use for an extra cooling, depuffing effect — especially wonderful after a long day.

Tips & Key Rules

Getting the most from a simple, powerful routine.

☀️
Add SPF — it's urgent
Your eye serum contains retinal, a potent retinoid. Without SPF, daytime retinal exposure can increase photosensitivity and worsen the exact concerns — pigmentation and ageing — you're trying to treat. SPF 50+ every morning is non-negotiable with retinoids in your routine.
💧
Apply serums to damp skin
The Make Me Wet serum and Centella ampoule both perform better on slightly damp skin. After cleansing, don't wait too long — apply while there's still a little moisture left. This helps aquaporin and hydrating actives draw moisture in rather than out.
🪨
Gua sha consistency is key
You won't see dramatic results from occasional use. Aim for 3–4 times per week consistently for 4–6 weeks and you'll notice real lifting, better circulation and reduced puffiness. Store in the fridge for the best experience.
🌿
Centella is your skin's best friend
The Skin 1004 Centella Ampoule can be used generously — treat it like a toner in the evening. On combination skin, it balances oily zones while nourishing dry patches. It's also the perfect product to reach for if skin ever feels irritated or reactive.
✨
Your routine is already anti-ageing
Retinal (eye serum), collagen (Medicube), peptides, aquaporin hydration, gua sha lifting, and centella barrier repair — every product in your collection actively targets ageing. You don't need more products; you need consistent daily use.
